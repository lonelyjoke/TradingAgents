### Company Quality Verdict

**Verdict: Below Average.** The company’s traditional core business, power and steam sales (85%+ of FY2025 revenue), is confirmed to be value-destructive or low-return. The -5.93% power sales gross margin in FY2025 is a hard, quantified fact. While the company talks about a strategic pivot to integrated energy services and AI, the audited financials show a business still overwhelmingly dependent on a struggling legacy segment. The complex web of related-party investment partnerships and high historical impairments (4.23Bn in FY2025 alone) signals poor past capital allocation and a corporate structure that benefits insiders. The lack of disclosed management direct shareholdings creates a principal-agent risk.

---

### Current Odds Verdict

**Verdict: Poor.** The market is pricing 002015.SZ for successful transformation: an 80x P/E TTM implies the market expects rapid, sustained profit growth from new ventures. Verified evidence contradicts this. Core earnings power is low and volatile (Q1’s profitability is heavily influenced by structural cost cuts and one-off items, not sustained core margin expansion). The FY2025 implosion in H2 margins (from 22.5% H1 to ~11% full year, implying near-zero or negative H2 gross margin) demonstrates a precedent for extreme profit volatility that the bull case ignores. The downside risk from even a modest re-rating to peer average multiples (P/E of 30-60x) or an FY2025-like margin collapse is substantial (potential downside of 40-50%). The probability of the bull case’s required 12-15Bn net profit materializing within the next 12 months is very low, given the absence of any quantified new business revenue.

---

### Relative Allocation Verdict

**Verdict: An inferior build candidate in its peer group.** 福能股份 (600483.SH) offers the same “新型电力” sector exposure with a dramatically better statistical profile: a 10.3x P/E, 1.06x P/B, lower leverage (40.4% debt), and a superior composite v4_score of 85.6 (vs. 002015.SZ’s 61.6). While 福能’s net profit is contracting, it isn’t starting from a position of negative core margins. The market’s assigned premium to 002015.SZ is entirely tethered to unquantified thematic optionality. For an investor seeking exposure to China’s energy transition, a peer like 福能股份 is a more statistically sound, lower-risk vehicle. The current price of 002015.SZ is not a good trade-off for the available evidence.

---

### Management & Capital Allocation Verdict

**Verdict: Distrustful.** The record is poor. High and persistent asset impairments reveal a multi-year pattern of value-destroying investment decisions. The FY2025 annual report notes impairments hit 4.23Bn due to “projects not meeting expected returns.” The complex, opaque related-party fund structures and high historical controlling shareholder pledge ratio (currently 21-27%, down from a peak of 48%) are a governance red flag. The decrease in 2026 pledge ratios is a marginal positive but does not erase the historical pattern. Without disclosed management direct equity holdings, there is minimal evidence that management’s interests are fully aligned with minority shareholders in a turnaround.

---

### Shareholder Structure Verdict

**Verdict: Concerning.** The shareholder base has fragmented significantly, with holder count exploding from ~50k in late 2024 to over 177k by 2026-03-31, a classic sign of retail-chasing-momentum. The float is dominated by the founding family (上海其辰 at 42.7%), leaving limited free float. The combination of a dominant, pledging insider and a rapidly retailizing shareholder base creates a volatile, event-risk prone ownership structure. While institutional funds (social security) appear in the top 10, their influence is dwarfed by the controlling shareholder.

---

### Execution & Falsification Plan

- **Core Bull Thesis**: The company successfully pivots to a high-margin “Green Power + AI Computing” platform, with FY2026H1 proving the sustainability of Q1’s margin improvement and showing initial quantified revenue from new ventures.
- **Primary Falsification Signal for Bulls**: If the 2026 half-year report (due August 2026) does **not** show a clear, sequential improvement in core power sales gross margin towards breakeven or positive, AND separately disaggregates less than 10% of total revenue from “算力” or new energy services, the thesis is invalidated.
- **Core Bear Thesis**: The Q1 2026 margin “improvement” was a one-off, masking a continued decline in the core power generation business, which will reassert itself, driving earnings back down and triggering a sharp multiple re-rating.
- **Primary Falsification Signal for Bears**: If Q2 2026 revenue stabilizes or grows sequentially, gross margin stays above 25%, and contract liabilities show a second consecutive quarter of growth, the bear thesis must be reconsidered.

**Trading Action for the Trader**:
**Reduce position to underweight.** The current risk/reward for holding a full weight is unacceptable. The market is pricing a future that has zero quantifiable evidence in current financials, while the confirmed past and present are of a low-quality, volatile, and unprofitable core business. Bank profits, and do not add until the August 2026 half-year report provides concrete, verifiable proof that the strategic transformation is generating sustainable, high-margin revenue.

**Next Data Point**: 2026 Half-Year Report (Late August 2026). Monitor for **1) Segment Revenue disclosure**, **2) Gross Margin for Power Sales**, and **3) Sustained sequential recovery in Contract Liabilities**.
