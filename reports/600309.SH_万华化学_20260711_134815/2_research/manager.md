# Research Manager Accepted Underwriting Model

**Rating**: Overweight

**Research Readiness**: partial

**Core Bet**: The core bet is that the structural shift in global MDI supply (driven by the permanent cost advantage of Chinese production over structurally impaired European capacity) and a permanent reduction in petrochemical costs (due to the Ethane cracker upgrade) will lead to a systematic upward shift in the earnings base. The current market price, anchored at a historically extreme 6.9% PB percentile and an 11.5x forward PE, is pricing in a sharp mean reversion of cyclical earnings to a depressed trough, underestimating the durability of the earnings upgrade.

### 本报告要回答的关键问题

1. Can the structural export competitiveness and current elevated margins in the Polyurethane (PU) segment be sustained, or is the current surge a transitory, crisis-driven spike?
2. Is the petrochemical cost reduction from the Ethylene cracker feedstock upgrade a permanent structural improvement to the earnings base, or a cyclical benefit that will revert?
3. What is the credible normalized earnings power of the entire enterprise over the next three years, and what valuation multiple should it command?
4. Do the company's balance sheet structure and capital allocation discipline provide a strong enough safety buffer to withstand a downturn and support valuation?

### 核心问题裁决

**Q1：Can the structural export competitiveness and current elevated margins in the PU segment be sustained?**
- **为何关键：** This is the dominant profit driver, contributing an estimated 70-80% of gross profit. The sustainability of PU exports determines whether 2026E earnings are a peak or a new normal.
- **当前结论：** The export surge is a composite of a structural shift and a cyclical crisis. The structural component (European de-industrialization, permanent cost disadvantage) is a multi-year driver. The crisis component (Middle East disruption) has amplified the speed and magnitude. Moderate reversion from the current 1.31w/ton MDI spread is likely, but the terminal spread will settle well above the previous cycle's trough due to true capacity exit.
- **采用证据：** KPE01: May MDI exports yoy+49%, cumulative ytd+16%, Q2 MDI spread 1.31w/ton.；KPE04: Wanhua's unit cost advantage >3000 CNY/ton vs global peers.；EV036: FY2025 overseas revenue growth +24% vs domestic +2%.；KPE03: Strait of Hormuz traffic at 60% of pre-crisis levels, indicating progressive normalization.
- **最强反证：** The 49% monthly export surge is clearly unsustainable and amplified by crisis-driven restocking. A rapid geopolitical resolution could cause a temporary volume and price air pocket as European production restarts and inventories are destocked.
- **模型/估值影响：** Base-case 2027E MDI spread lowered from initial bull's 1.2w/ton to a more conservative 1.1w/ton post-normalization. Reduced 2027E EPS by ~8 CNY cents vs the bull case.
- **置信度与下次验证：** medium；Monthly MDI/TDI export data for July and August 2026; H1 2026 semi-annual report segment revenue and margin disclosure.

**Q2：Is the petrochemical cost reduction from the Ethylene cracker feedstock upgrade a permanent structural improvement?**
- **为何关键：** This determines whether the second significant profit layer has seen a permanent step-change in its mid-cycle earnings power, or is merely enjoying a transient spread benefit.
- **当前结论：** The upgrade to allow Ethane cracking is a permanent structural improvement that lowers the cost curve, independent of the absolute level of the Ethane-Ethylene spread. This successfully de-risks and elevates the segment's earnings floor.
- **采用证据：** KPE01: Ethylene phase-1 diversity upgrade completed early 2026; Q2 Ethylene-Ethane spread 1003 USD/ton, QoQ +362 USD/ton.；KPE03: Shandong independent refineries utilization at record low 46.92%, indicating extreme demand weakness, which is a negative signal but does not invalidate the cost advantage.
- **最强反证：** The Q2 spread of 1003 USD/ton is likely unsustainably high and could revert severely. In a demand recession, the cost advantage would boost margins relative to competitors but would not prevent an overall profit decline.
- **模型/估值影响：** Accepted the structural nature of the cost advantage. Revised the long-term base-case Ethane-Ethylene spread down to a conservative 750 USD/ton, reflecting a durable but narrower cost advantage over the cycle.
- **置信度与下次验证：** high；Management commentary on cost savings and operating rates in H1 2026 report; FY2026 segment profitability disclosure.

**Q3：What is the credible normalized earnings power and its appropriate valuation multiple?**
- **为何关键：** This is the ultimate translation of segmental analysis into a forward expected return. The perception of a structural upgrade must justify a higher multiple than the market's cyclical peak fear.
- **当前结论：** Mid-cycle earnings power has demonstrably risen. The current valuation of ~11.5x forward PE and 1.93x PB (6.9% historical percentile) prices in a full reversion to pre-upgrade, deeply depressed trough earnings, creating a significant expectation gap.
- **采用证据：** KPE02: Wanhua PE ~12x, PB at the 10% percentile since 2020.；Market-Expectation Context: Implied TTM earnings very close to FY2025 trough profit; PB 6.9% percentile.；H1 2026 Guidance (EV023): 9.8-10.4 bn CNY parent profit.；Fundamental Analyst Memo: Base-case 2026E net profit 18.7 bn CNY.
- **最强反证：** Market participants, through the price action (60d -25% vs CSI300), are clearly pricing a sharp and imminent mean reversion in profits, treating the high current earnings as a peak. Their proxy is historical cyclical behavior.
- **模型/估值影响：** Base-case fair value set at 12x 2026E net profit, yielding a core equity value of 224.4 bn CNY, or ~71.7 CNY/share. This is consistent with the historical mid-cycle multiple, not an elevated one. Added a separate qualitative option value bucket for New Materials.
- **置信度与下次验证：** medium；Formal H1 2026 annual report in August, especially the consolidation of H2 guidance and segment details. Any monthly export data showing a sustained collapse.

**Q4：Do the company's balance sheet and capital allocation provide a strong safety buffer?**
- **为何关键：** A structural bull thesis requires survivability and rational capital management. High leverage and aggressive capex in a downturn could destroy value.
- **当前结论：** Balance sheet leverage is high but manageable. The significant improvement in OCF in 2026 will cover heavy capex. Management is not maximizing FCF but is strategically reinvesting with a proven track record, partially reflected in share buybacks. Strong OCF/NI ratio provides a cash-flow safety buffer.
- **采用证据：** Financial-report intelligence: FY2025 OCF/NI 2.64x; Asset-liability ratio 62.8%; FY2025 FCF only 2.4 bn CNY.；Management & Capital Allocation: Approved 1.25 CNY/share dividend for FY2025; active share buyback program in 2025.；Filing: R&D spending 4.865 bn CNY, demonstrating reinvestment.
- **最强反证：** Net debt of ~46 bn CNY and FCF of just 2.4 bn CNY in FY2025 illustrate that the model is very capital-intensive. An earnings downturn before capex moderates would quickly create a cash drain and stress the high debt load.
- **模型/估值影响：** Does not alter the core earnings multiple but caps the valuation multiple at the mid-cycle level (12x) rather than allowing expansion to a premium multiple. Constrains position size due to tail risk from leverage.
- **置信度与下次验证：** high；FY2026 annual report: track total debt, OCF, capex, and final FCF; observe if capex intensity moderates as projected.

## Company Disaggregation

Wanhua Chemical's economics are determined by three product units and two geographies. (1) Polyurethanes (MDI/TDI) is the core profit engine (approx. 70-80% of gross profit), driven by export volume and global product spreads. (2) Petrochemicals (Ethylene derivatives) is a profit enhancer whose structural margin has improved due to a permanent feedstock cost reduction. (3) New Materials (battery chemicals, PC) is a large-capital, R&D-intensive growth option, currently loss-making. Geographically, the Export segment is the critical growth driver, with revenue growth of +24% in FY2025 vs. +2% for Domestic. The structural investment case rests on the first two units and the Export geography.

## Debate Verdict

The bull scored a decisive point by correctly identifying a structural component behind the cyclical noise and having strong data (H1 guidance, export volumes) to prove current earnings power. The bear was correct in attacking the bull's extrapolation of peak quarterly earnings and forcing the debate to account for mean reversion and the capital-intensive nature of the business. The final verdict is a synthesis: the structural upgrade the bull argued for is real and underpriced, but the cycle is not dead. The bear successfully imposed logical discipline, ensuring the valuation does not extrapolate crisis-era spreads indefinitely. This synthesis results in an Overweight rating, anchored by a conservative mid-cycle multiple on a new, higher base of earnings.
