### PM Summary

The bear case (continued weak pig prices through Q3, inventory write-downs, cash burn, debt concerns) is equally plausible and more active in the near term. At 37.05 CNY (~2.55x PB, ~21.9x trailing PE), the risk/reward is approximately 1:1 – no edge for a strong directional bet.

This would invalidate the safety margin bulls rely on.
- **Next Verification Date:** Mid-June 2026 (MOA May livestock data, Muyuan May sales briefing). Key watch items: monthly pig price trajectory, sow herd inventory, cash cost per kg, contract liability conversion.
