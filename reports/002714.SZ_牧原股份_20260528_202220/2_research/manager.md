The company is a survivor and will emerge from this cycle stronger.

The bear thesis requires the less-heroic assumption of "no significant recovery" to deliver a 40-60% loss. The debate correctly identifies a symmetric outcome distribution around the current 37 CNY level, which does not constitute a compelling investment opportunity. - **The Bear** sees it as a passive, potentially dangerous accumulation of working capital, indicating that the cycle trough is deeper and longer than management expected, posing genuine refinancing and write-down risk.

**I rule for the Bear in the 3-6 month tactical window, but for the Bull in the 12-18 month strategic view.** The critical piece of missing evidence is a confirmed stabilization or uptick in pig prices. Without it, the bear case is not just a theoretical risk; it is the actively playing-out scenario, as confirmed by Q1 impairment charges and the price chart.

At 37.05 CNY, the market has not mispriced the stock for the current level of uncertainty. The price action is an "earnings-led drawdown," and the same-price history shows the current EPS support is *weaker* than prior episodes. The market is correctly pricing in deterioration. The **Expectation Gap is weak**—a 1:1 risk/reward is not a basis for a strong non-consensus bet.
