## Company Quality Verdict

**Verdict: Acceptable but unexceptional.** 沃尔核材 has real technical capabilities (224G mass production, 448G sampling), diversified end-markets (electronic communication + new energy power), and a long operating history. However, quality is dented by:

1. **Weak cash conversion**: Q1 OCF/net profit ratio of 0.2x is far below acceptable threshold for an industrial manufacturer.
2. **Opaque segment disclosure**: High-speed cable economics are not separately disclosed, making the core growth bet unverifiable from filings alone.
3. **Customer concentration risk**: Reliance on Amphenol, TE Connectivity, and Luxshare as primary customers gives limited pricing power as a tier-2 supplier.
4. **Heavy capex cycle**: 2025 capex of CNY 1,036mn (12.3% of revenue) in a year when OCF was CNY 1,160mn—almost all operating cash flow is being reinvested, with uncertain returns.

The company is not "broken"—it is a legitimate industrial technology firm. But it is **not a compounder** at this stage; it is a cyclical growth company in a heavy investment phase with unproven returns.
