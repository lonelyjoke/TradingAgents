### Expectation-Gap Evidence
- **Market's Implied View:** At ¥10.90 and 12.75× PE TTM, the market values the company at an implied TTM earnings of ~¥14.7 bn. This is 16% above the FY2025 actual (¥12.7 bn) but 27% below the Q1 2026 seasonality-adjusted annualized run-rate (≈¥20.1 bn).
- **The Gap:** The bear argues this 27% discount is justified because Q1 margins are a transient spread-driven peak—not a new structural plateau—and that receivables quality is deteriorating. The bull argues this discount is an overreaction, as the supply ceiling and cost improvements will sustain higher normalized earnings.
- **My Judgment:** The market's discount is directionally correct but perhaps slightly overdone in magnitude. A reasonable base-case normalized profit, assuming spreads compress but remain above the historical trough, might be ¥16–18 bn (8.5–11% ROE). At 12–14× on that, the stock is roughly fairly valued at ¥11–14, with downside risk if spreads compress faster and receivables turn. There is no compelling mispricing to exploit on the long side today. On the short/underweight side, the negative revision path (spread compression → margin contraction → earnings cuts → derating) offers a clearer near-term catalyst trajectory.
- **Valuation Positions:** PE at 22nd percentile (low), but PB at 85th and PS at 90th percentile (high). This classic late-cycle configuration shows the market already distrusts the current earnings stream. Until ROE durability is proven over multiple quarters, the PB/PS elevation acts as a ceiling, not a floor.
- **Price-EPS-PE Decomposition:** The 1-year return (+63%) was primarily a double-engine of EPS growth (+7%) and multiple expansion (+52.5%). The 3-year and 5-year windows were earnings-led. The recent sharp drop, against a modest commodity decline, suggests the multiple-expansion engine is now reversing as the market questions earnings durability. This is a fragile setup.

### Company Quality Verdict
中国铝业 is a **medium-to-high-quality integrated aluminum producer** with genuine scale advantages, a strategic position in China's aluminum supply chain, and a massively improved balance sheet versus a decade ago.
- **Strengths:** Industry-leading scale, vertically integrated (bauxite → alumina → aluminum), cash flow generation has improved markedly (OCF 1.97× net profit in Q1 2026), leverage is moderate (debt-to-assets ~43%), and shareholder returns have been initiated (dividends, buyback proposals).
- **Weaknesses:** Profitability is structurally lower-margin than best-in-class peer 云铝股份 (gross margin 25.8% vs 31.6%; ROE 17.6% vs 20.1%). The business remains deeply cyclical, and evidence of "structural" margin lift is confined to a single quarter.
- **Conclusion:** A good company, not a great one. It deserves to trade at a discount to the highest-quality aluminum peers, which is what the current relative valuation shows (slightly higher PE, lower PB premium on ROE).

## 安全价格区间 / 防御性建仓锚

**结论：基于当前可得的证据，可以为 601600.SH 设定一个宽泛的防御性建仓观察区间，但该区间的可靠性为中等偏低，不构成即刻买入的建议。**

**安全价格区间：¥7.50 – ¥9.00**

**估值桥梁：**
- **周期谷底盈利假设：** 假设铝价跌回¥20,000/吨，铝土矿/氧化铝/电力成本回归历史均值，公司年化归母净利润回到¥100-120亿区间（参考FY2025的¥127亿并略微下调，因为当时包含了部分高位铝价）。这对应EPS为¥0.58-0.70/股。
- **估值锚：** 在盈利下行周期，资源股通常交易在10-15倍PE。取区间中下沿11-13倍PE，安全价格对应¥7.50-9.10。
- **PB锚：** 当前每股净资产约为¥4.70（10.90 / 2.32 PB）。在极端悲观情景下，若ROE从当前的17.6%大幅回落至8-10%，PB可能压缩至1.6-1.9倍，对应价格¥7.50-8.90。
- **股息锚：** 即使盈利下滑，公司维持每股¥0.10-0.12的分红（基于降低后的派息率），在¥7.50-9.00区间内，股息率约为1.3-1.6%，这提供了微弱的支撑，但不足以构成主要安全边际。

**必须保持成立的商业条件：**
1.  **产能天花板必须稳固：** 中国政府对4500万吨电解铝产能红线的政策承诺不能出现松动。
2.  **SHFE铝价不出现崩溃：** 铝价必须维持在¥19,000/吨以上，任何跌破此水平都意味着全球需求萎缩或国内供给端出现意外放松。
3.  **资产负债表不出险：** 公司净负债水平（计息负债-现金）不能大幅攀升，经营性现金流/净利润比率需维持在1.0倍以上。
4.  **无大规模资产减值：** 长期股权投资和海外矿山不能出现巨额减值。

**缓慢建仓计划（仅当价格进入区间且基本面信号改善时考虑）：**
1.  **观察期（¥9.00-10.00）：** 等待Q2财报验证毛利率和应收账款趋势。如果毛利率稳定在21%以上且应收/营收比率不再恶化至4%以上，可将该区间设定为小规模试探性建仓区。

**使安全价格失效的恶化条件：**

- 公司季度毛利率跌破18%且无反弹迹象。

**结论重申：在¥10.90的现价下，股票未进入安全区间，且面临盈利预测下修、估值收缩和机构减仓的三重逆风。当前策略应为Underweight，而非防守性建仓。**
