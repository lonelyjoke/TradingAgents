### **Core Thesis Deconstruction: Why the Bull Case Falters**

The bull’s core bet is that **“core operating profit, stripping out FX losses, will be robust and drive re-rating.”** This is now a low-confidence bet reliant on three weakening pillars.

#### **1. The “Normalizing Growth” Narrative is Unproven and Risky**

The bull’s central argument hinges on the idea that Q1 2026 revenue growth of 14.82% YoY is healthy after an “anomalous” Q4 2025, and that a strong re-acceleration is imminent. This is the most critical and most vulnerable assumption.

- **The “Seasonal Interruption” Defense is a Statistical Illusion:** The bull argues Q4 2025 revenue (2.888B CNY) was an accounting-driven anomaly, implying a normalized H2 2025 run-rate. This is a dangerous inference. Even if we grant this, the combined Q4 2025 + Q1 2026 revenue is 8.76B CNY. The company needs a truly exceptional H2 2026, driven by unproven new ventures (Vietnam, software), to re-accelerate the full-year growth rate meaningfully above the current ~15%. The filing evidence confirms Q4 is seasonally weaker (Q1 5.11B, Q2 6.63B, Q3 6.65B, Q4 2.89B in 2025), but calling it an “anomaly” to dismiss a deceleration signal is an act of pure, unverified inference.
- **The Inventory Signal is a Real Drag, Not a Bull Flag:** The bull argues that a 107% increase in contract liabilities justifies a 72% increase in inventory. This is a logical but incomplete defense. The **inventory turnover ratio is the critical missing evidence**. The stark fact is that after the bull’s “front-loaded demand” quarter (Q1 2026), inventory remains elevated at 3.052B CNY. This means the inventory wasn't efficiently converted into revenue. The company is sitting on a mountain of stock, tying up its otherwise impressive 4.44B CNY operating cash flow. The risk of a future impairment or discounting cycle is increasing, directly threatening gross margins (already a moderate 26.54%).

#### **2. The “Software Re-Rating” Fantasy is Premature and Overstated**

The bull pins part of its thesis on a valuation re-rating from a ~48M CNY software service fee. This is a classic narrative fallacy.

- **Revenue Contribution is Immaterial:** Annualized at ~2B CNY, this is less than 1% of total 2025 sales. It will be lost in the noise of a 21.3B CNY revenue base for the foreseeable future.
- **No Filing-Based Valuation Bridge:** The **Thematic Valuation Bridge** correctly classifies this as a **“tier-3 narrative option (small imagination premium only).”** There is no segment reporting, no disclosed margin profile, and no contracted backlog for this software revenue. The bull is asking us to price in a SaaS multiple before any proof of a standalone, high-margin, recurring revenue stream exists. This is pure optionality and cannot justify an Overweight or Buy call on a stock facing core business headwinds.
- **Anecdotal Evidence Is Not Enough:** The exchange interaction confirming the 48M figure is a single, unquantified data point. It is not a verified, audited growth vector from an annual report. We cannot buy a stock on a “tier-3” catalyst.

#### **3. The Real Core Problem: The Vanishing Earnings Bridge**

The bull’s entire stance collapses when we apply the **Earnings-Model Discipline**. The bull cannot build a credible bridge to double-digit EPS growth for the next 2-3 quarters.
- The **market expectation snapshot** shows implied TTM earnings are very close to the last annual profit (1.17x), but far above the seasonality-adjusted Q1 2026 profit estimate (0.79x). The market is already pricing in a recovery from a depressed Q1. The bull’s “base case” of FX normalization leading to an 18-20B CNY profit is already what the market is pricing. **The “expectation gap” is actually closed, not wide open.**
- The **Price-EPS-PE Decomposition** confirms the danger. The 1-year price move (-41.5%) is a **“derating despite EPS growth”** story, but over the last 6 months, it has transitioned to a **“double drag: EPS decline plus multiple contraction.”** The market is not stupid; it’s reacting to a forward-looking EPS cliff that the bull is dismissing.
