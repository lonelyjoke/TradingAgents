### **Decisive Evidence & Unchanged Core Facts**

However, the debate successfully exposed that **this operating recovery is being financed by a significant deterioration in balance sheet quality and cash conversion, while the market's valuation already prices in a level of durable, high-quality growth that has not materialized.**

The decisive evidence that shifts the balance towards the bear thesis is threefold:
1.  **A Synchronous "Phantom" Cycle:** The bull correctly noted gross margins improved by ~2.6pp. The bear correctly noted that this "value creation" is captured almost entirely by ballooning working capital. Accounts Receivable stands at a colossal 55.8% of annualized revenue, and prepayments surged 59.5%. The resulting Operating Cash Flow to Net Profit ratio is a stark **-1.70x**. This means the operating recovery is actively consuming cash, a fact the bull never adequately neutralized.

Importantly, the shares were transferred at a very low price (CNY 9.22), meaning the new holders are sitting on massive gains and have a strong incentive to sell, creating a future overhang. Meanwhile, the number of holders exploded from ~197k to ~693k, a feature of weak institutional sponsorship and likely future selling pressure.
3.  **The Unconquered "Research Black Hole":** The bull’s thesis that R&D spending (CNY 43.4B in 2025, 1.56x net profit) is building an impenetrable “moat” was not proven. The bear successfully reframed this as a structural expense problem. By comparing it to the higher-margin, lower-R&D, and faster-growing peer 三一重能 (688349.SH), the bear demonstrated that the prodigious R&D spending has not translated into superior unit economics or profitability, undermining the case for a “quality premium” valuation.
