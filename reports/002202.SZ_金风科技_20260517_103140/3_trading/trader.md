**Gap**: The decisive missing link is *profit quality* – whether rising contract liabilities (+65.5% YoY to ¥19.76bn) will translate into expanding gross margins, and whether negative OCF (-1.7x net profit) is a timing issue or structural.

**Classification**: **High valuation / low prosperity** – 34x PE, 94.7% PB percentile, yet margins and cash flow are compressing. The current price demands a near-perfect margin/cash-flow recovery that is not yet evidenced.
