### 核心问题裁决

**Q1：Can the memory ASP super-cycle extend to 2027?**
- **为何关键：** Determines base case net profit trajectory and duration of earnings peak; pivotal for valuation and position sizing.
- **当前结论：** Unresolved, but improving. Q1 gross margin (57.1%) and contract liability growth are hard confirmations of a boom, but the evidence for extension to 2027 relies on unverified channel checks. The missing link is Q3/H2 forward pricing data.
- **采用证据：** EV013: Q1 2026 gross margin 57.1%；KPE01: DRAM channel checks indicating sustained tight supply；KPE03: TrendForce bullish 2027 DRAM outlook；EV010-EV015: Q1 2026 financials confirming strong revenue and profitability
- **最强反证：** The bear argues channel-check sources are unverified and the market is already pricing a sharp peak, evidenced by a -45% drawdown and two consecutive limit-downs, reflecting a 'sentiment kill'.
- **模型/估值影响：** Base case 2026E net profit 4,185 mn CNY (EPS 5.97). Bull scenario (30% probability) raises net profit to 7,000 mn, increasing probability-weighted fair value by ~50%. Bear scenario (20%) drops net profit to 3,500 mn, reducing fair value to ~160 CNY.
- **置信度与下次验证：** medium；2026 semi-annual report (August): Q2 gross margin ≥55% and forward guidance on Q3 ASP/order trends.

**Q2：Is the 57% gross margin and 8.6% R&D ratio sustainable?**
- **为何关键：** Margin durability defines the slope of operating leverage and determines whether current profitability reflects a structural shift or a cyclical top.
- **当前结论：** Partially resolved in bear's favor. The 57% GM likely benefits from cost lag and high utilization; the 8.6% R&D ratio is a function of surging revenue, not a permanent cost cut. Base case assumes R&D ratio normalizes to 12% in 2027E.
- **采用证据：** EV013: Q1 2026 gross margin 57.1%；EV015: R&D to revenue ratio 8.6% in Q1 2026；Historical segment cost structure (limited disclosure)
- **最强反证：** The bull claims the ratios are structural improvements, but semi-fixed R&D expense and fabless cost pass-through make the ratios highly sensitive to revenue growth; a single-digit revenue decline could compress margins sharply.
- **模型/估值影响：** Each 1% decline in 2027E gross margin reduces net profit by ~184 mn CNY. A normalization of R&D to sales from 8.6% to 12% on ~100 bn H2 2026E revenue creates a ~3.4 bn profit headwind. The base case models a gradual reversion.
- **置信度与下次验证：** medium；2026 H1 absolute R&D costs; if costs drop sharply, bull thesis is strengthened; if absolute amount stays flat, confirms cyclicality.

**Q3：Does the balance sheet provide a concrete downside floor?**
- **为何关键：** In a sharp re-rating, asset quality determines permanent loss risk and position sizing under worst-case scenarios.
- **当前结论：** Confirmed. Both sides agree on financial strength. Net cash of 146.9 bn CNY, zero short-term borrowing, and strong cash conversion are verified facts.
- **采用证据：** EV016: 2025 annual report net cash 146.9 bn CNY；EV018: debt-to-assets 8.1%；EV019: cash conversion metrics
- **最强反证：** The bear notes that a fortress balance sheet does not prevent multiple compression during a sector rotation; the stock can still test lower multiples in a risk-off event.
- **模型/估值影响：** Downside floor is defined by asset value, not dynamic P/E. At a trough PB of 4x on BVPS ~40 CNY, theoretical floor is ~160 CNY. This invalidates the bear's worst-case 200 CNY as a permanent loss scenario.
- **置信度与下次验证：** high；If the stock approaches 160 CNY, verify BVPS and net cash; any significant deterioration in net cash would undermine the floor.
