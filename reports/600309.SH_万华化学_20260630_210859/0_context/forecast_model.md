Revenue growth historically driven by volume expansion (new capacity) and price cycles.

Parent Net Profit = Operating Profit - finance costs ± non-recurring items - minority interest - tax; Q1 2026 net margin = 6.88% (-0.28pp YoY). FY2025 parent net profit = 12.527 bn CNY. Profit swings driven primarily by MDI price/spread cycles and Petrochemical cracker profitability.
- Cash flow: OCF = Parent Net Profit + D&A ± working capital changes; Q1 2026 OCF/NI ratio = 1.84x (strong). FCF = OCF - maintenance capex - expansion capex. Working-capital efficiency improving: Receivables/Rev = 6.96% (-1.27pp YoY), Inventory/Rev = 13.29% (-2.77pp YoY). High expansion capex load expected (chemical investment cycle) partially offset by healthy operating cash generation.
- Reinvestment: Asset-heavy, high fixed-cost operating leverage. Total assets ≈ 216 bn CNY, total liabilities ≈ 142 bn CNY (implied debt-to-assets ~66%; peer comparison gives 64.32%). FY2025 R&D expense = 4.865 bn CNY. Heavy capex cycle continues for POE, battery materials, and petrochemical integration; ROIC highly dependent on cycle margins and utilization on new capacity.
