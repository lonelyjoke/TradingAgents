## Core Thesis & Verdict

The bull’s core bet was that an “earnings elasticity” cycle would drive a non-linear profit recovery and re-rate the stock. This thesis **is falsified** on two unverified assumptions:

1. **Q1 2026 operating profit of 18.2B RMB (+109%)** — explicitly admitted by the bull analyst as an inference, not a fact from official filings.  
2. **Wind turbine bid prices rebounding to 1,600–1,700 yuan/kW** — no supporting data provided; bear correctly highlighted the research gap.

Because these two assumptions are the **foundation** of the margin-expansion and operating-leverage narrative, the bull case cannot stand. Without them, the only verified profitability metric is a **net margin of ~4.4%**, which is *contracting* relative to revenue growth (+63.5% revenue vs +59.6% net profit).

The bear’s counter-narrative is built entirely on **verified facts from the debate record**:

- Absolute AR growth exceeds absolute net profit each quarter → credit-driven revenue, not cash-generating growth.
- Q1 2026 operating cash flow is negative → profitless prosperity.
- Net profit growth trailing revenue growth → margin compression, not leverage.
- Market regime extremes: CSI 300 PE at 98.4th percentile, Electrical Equipment turnover at 90.9th percentile → extreme crowding, high downside risk.
