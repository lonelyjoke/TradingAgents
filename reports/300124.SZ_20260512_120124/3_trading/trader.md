## Core Bet & Expectation Gap

- **Bull thesis (implicit in price):** The market is buying a V-shaped recovery in earnings, an H-share re-rating catalyst, and a United Power spin-off value unlock. This requires three unverified events to coincide—a low-probability chain.
- **Bear thesis (verified):** 2026Q1 net profit collapsed -23% YoY, gross margin dropped 340bp to 29.1%, and revenue growth halved to 13%. The most recent hard data is severely negative.
- **Expectation gap:** At ¥77.49 (44x TTM P/E), the stock already prices in a *modest* recovery. The market has rallied +26% from the April low *without* any fundamental improvement—this rebound rests on speculation, not on new operating data.

---
