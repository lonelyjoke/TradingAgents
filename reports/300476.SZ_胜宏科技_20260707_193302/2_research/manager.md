# Research Manager Accepted Underwriting Model

**Rating**: Hold

**Research Readiness**: partial

**Core Bet**: AI PCB revenue mix shift to 60-70% and consolidated gross margin durability above 34%.

### 本报告要回答的关键问题

1. Can AI PCB revenue share reach 60-70% in FY26, and what is the segment margin?
2. Can consolidated gross margin stay above 34% through FY26 despite upstream cost pressure and potential client pricing pressure?
3. Will the 18bn CNY FY26 capex translate into proportionate revenue growth by FY27, or will the "slow ramp" compress returns?
4. Is customer concentration risk (single GPU client) being mitigated by ASIC/TPU diversification at a material pace?

### 核心问题裁决

**Q1：Can AI PCB revenue share reach 60-70% in FY26, and what is the segment margin?**
- **为何关键：** AI mix determines blended ASP, gross margin, and revenue growth trajectory; without official segment disclosure the core thesis is unverifiable.
- **当前结论：** Bullish scenario is plausible but unverified. Accepted base case: 60% AI share, AI GM ~36%, consolidated GM 34.0%.
- **采用证据：** KPE02 private channel guidance AI share 60-70%；Q1 2026 gross margin 34.46%；FY25 revenue +80% YoY
- **最强反证：** No official segment revenue/margin disclosure; KPE02 is unverified private data; inventory/revenue up to 17.7% may signal demand not fully absorbed.
- **模型/估值影响：** 1pp AI share = 276mn revenue; 1pp AI GM = 2.8mn gross profit per 1% of revenue; base case AI share 60% vs. bull 70% reduces FY26E revenue by ~2.8bn and net profit by ~0.8bn.
- **置信度与下次验证：** medium；Official segment disclosure in 2026 semi-annual report (expected August 2026).

**Q2：Can consolidated gross margin stay above 34% through FY26 despite upstream cost pressure and potential client pricing pressure?**
- **为何关键：** Gross margin is the primary profit driver; a 1pp move changes net profit by ~1.4bn CNY, directly shifting scenario fair values.
- **当前结论：** Bull has stronger near-term evidence (Q1 2026 actual 34.46% is a hard fact). Bear's cost pressure (electronic cloth +100%, inventory build) is a medium-term risk not yet visible in P&L. Base case 34.0% embeds 50bp caution buffer.
- **采用证据：** Q1 2026 gross margin 34.46%；KPE02 'costs stable, pass-through smooth'；KPE01 electronic cloth +100% YoY to 7.4 CNY/meter；Q1 2026 inventory/revenue 17.7% (+4.2pp YoY), contract liabilities -37%
- **最强反证：** KPE01 upstream cost spike and KPE10 (unverified) Nvidia 10% price cut rumour; if confirmed, margin could drop below 33%.
- **模型/估值影响：** GM 34.0% (base) vs. 33.0% (bear) → net profit difference ~1.4bn CNY; bear case implies equity value down to 135bn CNY (-52% vs. market cap).
- **置信度与下次验证：** medium；Q2 2026 gross margin disclosure in interim report (August 2026).

**Q3：Will the 18bn CNY FY26 capex translate into proportionate revenue growth by FY27, or will the "slow ramp" compress returns?**
- **为何关键：** 18bn capex is 93% of FY25 revenue; if ramp is slow, ROIC deteriorates and depreciation drags on margins in FY27-28, destroying value.
- **当前结论：** Bear wins on timing. Official disclosure 'long construction cycles, gradual ramp' (2026-06-08) is higher-evidence than private channel optimism. FY27E revenue growth reduced to 25% (from 30% base), FY27E net profit reduced to 6.30bn CNY.
- **采用证据：** Official investor interaction 2026-06-08: 'PCB heavy asset, long construction cycles, gradual capacity ramp'；KPE02: factory 4 in mass production, factories 10/11 progressing；Q1 2026 construction-in-progress 5.17bn CNY (+43% QoQ)
- **最强反证：** KPE02 claims factory 4 already ramped and factories 10/11 on schedule; OCF 1.64x supports funding.
- **模型/估值影响：** FY27E net profit lowered from 7.72bn to 6.30bn; equity value at 35x PE reduced by ~17bn CNY.
- **置信度与下次验证：** high；FY26 annual report capex detail and FY27 H1 revenue growth vs. 25% assumption.

**Q4：Is customer concentration risk (single GPU client) being mitigated by ASIC/TPU diversification at a material pace?**
- **为何关键：** Single-customer concentration is the top qualitative risk; successful diversification supports multiple expansion and extends the growth runway beyond the current GPU cycle.
- **当前结论：** Bullish optionality is credible (V8 TPU entry, 50/50 target) but unverified. No official customer split or contract wins are disclosed.
- **采用证据：** KPE02 private channel: target ASIC/GPU 50/50, confident in V8 TPU PCB supply by FY26 year-end
- **最强反证：** No official customer disclosure; ASIC margins unproven; timeline uncertain.
- **模型/估值影响：** Diversification supports 30% bull probability and potential P/E expansion to 40x; without it, concentration risk caps multiple and bear-case probability would rise.
- **置信度与下次验证：** low；Customer announcements, contract wins, or FY26 annual report customer concentration disclosure change.

## Forecast Takeaways

### 预测take-aways

**1. Revenue growth remains strong but is slowing from peak. FY26E 27.6bn CNY (+43% YoY) decelerates to +25%/+20% in FY27-28 as the base effect normalizes. The bull's 55% FY26 growth is unlikely given management's own 'slow ramp' caution.**
- 证据锚：FY25A revenue 19.3bn, Q1 2026 5.52bn (run-rate ~22bn). FY26 guidance implies H2 acceleration; achievable but not guaranteed.
- 财务含义：FY26E revenue 27.6bn, FY27E 32.3bn, FY28E 38.8bn. Slower ramp reduces FY27-28 net profit by ~1.5bn vs. prior base.
- 置信度与风险：Confidence: medium. Top-down, supported by KPE02 guidance but missing hard backlog data. Invalidation: FY26 revenue below 25bn CNY.

**2. Gross margin resilience is the linchpin of the investment case. Q1 2026 34.5% is a positive signal, but one quarter is not a trend. Upstream cost pressure could compress margins in H2 2026 even if AI ASP is stable.**
- 证据锚：Q1 2026 GM 34.46%; Q2 2026 is the decisive verification. KPE01 cost signals and KPE10 pricing rumor are flagged.
- 财务含义：Base case FY26E GM 34.0% embeds 50bp caution. A fall to 33.0% would cut FY26E net profit by ~1.0bn CNY.
- 置信度与风险：Confidence: moderate. Supported by Q1 actual, challenged by cost data. Invalidation: Q2 2026 GM below 33%.

**3. Capacity ramp is a 2027-28 story, not a 2026 story. Official disclosure confirms 'long construction cycles, gradual ramp.' The 18bn CNY capex will start contributing meaningfully only from FY27, and depreciation will be a headwind.**
- 证据锚：Official Q&A 2026-06-08; Q1 2026 construction-in-progress 5.17bn CNY (+43% QoQ).
- 财务含义：FY27 revenue growth reduced to 25%; FY28 operating margin reduced to 25.5% on depreciation. FCF remains negative through FY27.
- 置信度与风险：Confidence: high. Official disclosure trumps private narrative. Invalidation: FY27 revenue growth exceeds 35%.

## Ranked Core Theses

- **证伪条件：** Q2 2026 GM <33%; AI revenue share <50% in official disclosure.
- **当前裁决：** unproven

### 论点2：ASIC/TPU diversification from single GPU client to multiple CSP programs (V8 TPU entry targeted FY26 end) reduces concentration risk and extends the growth runway.

- **当前裁决：** unproven

### 论点3：18bn CNY FY26 capex faces 'long construction cycles, gradual ramp' (official disclosure). Depreciation will compress margins in FY27-28. If revenue ramp lags, ROIC will be poor, and bear-case downside is -52%.

## Thesis-to-Financial Bridge

## Moat Evidence Verdict

Partial moat evidence. Transition from commodity PCB to high-end AI PCB is supported by high R&D spend (7.78bn CNY FY25, +73% YoY), management-claimed share in key GPU client's PCB basket, and Q1 2026 gross margin 34.5%. However, independent third-party verification of technology leadership is missing. Customer co-design and mSAP/HDI capability are plausible barriers, but single-client concentration and unverified capacity ramp returns limit moat durability. Counterevidence: competitor 沪电股份 has similar margins and lower valuation. Moat supports premium margins today but is not yet fully proven; official segment disclosure is required to confirm share, pricing power, and retention.

## Valuation Closure

Mutually exclusive scenarios:
- Base case (50%): FY27E NP 6.30bn, terminal PE 35x → equity value 269bn CNY.
- Bull case (30%): FY27E NP 7.50bn (fast ramp, diversification), PE 40x → 420bn CNY.
- Bear case (20%): FY27E NP 5.00bn, PE 25x → 135bn CNY.
Probability-weighted equity value: 0.3×420 + 0.5×269 + 0.2×135 = 288.1bn CNY.
Current market cap 280bn CNY → expected return ~3%. No per-share fair value due to missing diluted share count. Double-counting checks: AI segment contribution embedded in consolidated; optionality (Thailand/Vietnam) not separately valued; no off-balance-sheet items. Valuation supports Hold rating: insufficient upside for Overweight, but not overvalued enough for Underweight.
