# Investment Memo: 000967.SZ (盈峰环境)

**Rating: Sell**  
**Current Price: 14.83 yuan**  
**Conviction: High (evidence-limited due to missing micro data, but directional thesis robust)**

---

## 1. Core Bet & Expectation Gap

**Core bearish bet:** The market is paying ~83x trailing P/E for a company with a 1.24% ROE, 52 billion yuan goodwill, 61 billion yuan receivables, and no proven operating model transition. The bet is that valuation will revert to a level consistent with current financial fragility — i.e., significant downside exists unless profitability structurally improves.

**Expectation gap:** The market appears to be pricing in a successful transformation from equipment maker to high-margin service operator, yet no micro-level evidence (service contract margins, NEV penetration rates, operating leverage) supports this. The "strong" operating cash flow (1.77x net income) is largely working-capital-driven, not indicative of sustainable earnings quality.

## 3. Critical Missing Evidence (research gap)

- **Service contract gross margin:** No disclosed data on profitability of the "service operator" pivot. Without this, the transformation thesis is untestable.
- **Goodwill impairment test details:** The annual report did not include explicit, granular impairment test results. **This is the single most important risk factor.** An adverse outcome could erase a material portion of book value.
- **NEV sanitation vehicle penetration rate and margin:** Ambiguous whether this growth vector is actually improving unit economics.

**Impact:** These gaps cap confidence in any bullish scenario. The bearish case, however, relies little on these — it only needs to assert that current valuation is unjustified by the *available* weak data.

## 4. Risk/Reward Calibration

**Probability-weighted expected return (own assessment):**

| Scenario | Probability | Target Price | Return |
|----------|------------|--------------|--------|
| **Pessimistic:** Growth disappoints, PE compresses to 25x (in line with lower-quality cyclicals). | 40% | ~10 yuan | -33% |
| **Neutral:** Revenue grows 10-15%, but no margin improvement. PE drifts to 30x (still rich vs. sector). | 45% | ~12-13 yuan | -12% to -18% |
| **Optimistic:** All bull assumptions materialize; PE stays >50x. | 15% | ~20 yuan | +35% |
| **Expected value (probability-weighted):** **~ -10% to -12% negative expected return** | | | |

**Key risk vs. payoff asymmetry:** Max upside (+35%) requires multiple low-probability events simultaneously. Downside (-33%) is both more likely and easier to trigger — just a normal earnings miss or negative special item.

**Calibration to market/sector:** Market mood is risk-on, but environmental services are not high-beta growth darlings. The sector is capital-intensive, low-margin, and policy-dependent. The stock’s extreme valuation (83x vs. sector 11x) makes it a **high valuation / low prosperity** setup. This combination rarely ends well without a concrete catalyst — which the bull case lacks.

## 5. Action & Position Sizing

**Action:** **Sell** any existing position immediately. For new entrants, do not initiate.

**Re-entry watch range:** **10-12 yuan** (implying ~25-30x P/E on likely 2025 profit of ~7-8B yuan) — but **only** if the following conditions concurrently become verifiable:

1. Q2 2026 report shows net margin >5.5% (not just revenue growth).
2. Goodwill impairment test is published with clear, positive conclusions and detailed assumptions.
3. Service contract gross margin is disclosed and exceeds 25% (a threshold that would indicate real margin improvement).

Until then, the risk/reward is unattractive.

---

## 6. Catalysts & Falsification Signals

**Catalysts to watch (bearish triggers):**
- Any negative surprise in interim earnings (especially margin contraction).
- Announcement of goodwill impairment (even partial).
- Management commentary that tempers growth expectations.
- Major shareholder selling after forced conversion expiry.

**Falsification signals (what would change the view to neutral/bullish):**
- Sustained improvement in reported ROE above 4%.
- Declining receivables/goodwill relative to market cap.
- Evidence of operating leverage (revenue up 20%+ while costs grow slower).
- Strong contract wins with disclosed margin details.

---

## 7. Summary

The stock is priced for a perfect transformation that is neither verified nor likely given the underlying financial structure. The bear case is grounded in verified data (low ROE, high risk assets, negative free cash flow) and a realistic probability model. **Hold is inappropriate** because the bull and bear evidence is not balanced — the bull case relies on unverified assumptions while the bear case rests on observable financial fragility. **Sell** is the only consistent rating.
