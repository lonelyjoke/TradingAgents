- Revenue: Revenue = Σ (wafer shipments per node × ASP per wafer) + Other service/IP revenue; driven by capacity, utilisation and process mix.
- Profit: Gross profit = Revenue − (Materials + Labour + Depreciation + Energy + Other manufacturing costs); Operating profit = Gross profit − R&D − SG&A; EBITDA margin ≈57.3% (Q1 2026, IFRS basis).
- Cash flow: OCF ≈ Net profit + Depreciation ± Working capital; FCF = OCF − Capex; Q1 2026 OCF/Net profit = 3.77x reflects heavy depreciation add-back.
- Reinvestment: High: annual capex typically exceeds depreciation; expansion projects in Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenzhen; no dividend for FY2025 to preserve cash for capacity build-out.
