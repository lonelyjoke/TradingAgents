Even optimistic scenarios (18 bn net profit, 50x PE) imply fair value ~50.8 yuan — well below current price.

**Expectation gap:** The market has priced a strong cyclical recovery, but the core catalyst (separator price stabilisation) remains a **verified gap**. Downside risk (balance sheet, valuation compression) outweighs plausible upside given current information.

Upstream costs: polyolefin resins, machinery. Capacity expansions (from Enjie, rivals) are announced but utilisation rates are opaque. Global EV sales growth is moderating but still positive. **Without micro price data, macro EV demand does not directly confirm margin inflection.** A 20% utilisation increase could raise EBITDA sharply even without price recovery, but that is a *cost leverage* effect, not a *pricing power* one.
