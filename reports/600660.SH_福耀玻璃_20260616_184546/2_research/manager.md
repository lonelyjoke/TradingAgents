Thematic elements like the “EU Industrial Accelerator Act” and “Product Mix Upgrade (panoramic roofs, HUD)” are **scenario value / qualitative optionality**. They strengthen the bull case by providing a durable demand and margin tailwind, but they are not the primary reason for the immediate Overweight rating. The rating is driven by the core operating engine’s resilience at a trough valuation.

**Exceptional.** 福耀玻璃 is a global manufacturing leader with an estimated ~30% market share, a 25%+ ROE, and superior profitability metrics (37.4% gross margin) versus any domestic peer. Its moat is built on scale, customer integration, and technology that competitors cannot easily replicate. The company converts profits into cash reliably (FY2025 OCF/NI of 1.29x) and has a 25-year track record of consistent, growing dividends. This is a top-tier industrial compounder.

### Industry Cycle Verdict
**Structurally growing, not cyclically peak.**
福耀 is a derivative of the auto industry, not a commodity glass producer. The industry cycle scan’s “range-bound / bottom-testing” label refers to broad materials; for 福耀, the key cycle is the penetration of high-value-added glass. This is a structural upgrade cycle, not a boom-bust one. The demand driver (more glass per vehicle, smarter glass) is in an early-to-mid stage, providing a decade-long growth runway that is largely independent of global auto production volumes.

### Business Model / Segment Economics Verdict
**Simple, scaled, and defensible.**
- **Core Engine (>85% of revenue):** Automotive glass for OEMs. High switching costs, long qualification cycles, and intimate co-design processes create a deep moat. Pricing is indexed to product complexity, not raw material spot prices, giving it unique margin stability.
- **Second Curve (Embedded in core):** The integration of glass with aluminum trim, sensors, and seals into a “glass module.” This raises ASP and strengthens the moat, acting as a same-customer margin expansion story, not a risky new venture.
- **Float Glass:** Vertically integrated to ensure supply and manage the quality of a key input, not operated as a standalone profit center. This buffers the core business from upstream price volatility.
