1. Can Fenjiu maintain earnings quality in the industry downturn?

### 核心问题裁决

**Q1：Can Fenjiu maintain earnings quality in the industry downturn?**
- **为何关键：** If cash conversion deteriorates or margins collapse beyond 72%, the bear-case fair value drops to ~70 CNY.
- **当前结论：** Earnings quality remains pristine (OCF/NP 1.53x, zero receivables), but gross margin declined 3.75pp, shrinking absolute profit; quality does not offset top-line pressure.
- **采用证据：** EV013: Q1 2026 gross margin 74.25% vs 78% YoY；EV010: Q1 OCF 10.5 bn, OCF/NP 1.53x；EV012: contract liabilities 7.9 bn (+35.9% YoY)
- **最强反证：** Gross margin erosion is accelerating in Qinghua 30, potentially dragging blended margin below 72% if industry downturn persists.
- **模型/估值影响：** Keeps base gross margin assumption at 74.25% for 2026E; bear case uses 72%. Each 1pp margin change shifts net profit by ~0.35 bn.
- **置信度与下次验证：** high；2026 H1 report gross margin (expected end-August).

**Q2：Is channel inventory a ticking time bomb?**
- **为何关键：** A sudden destocking event could cause revenue to drop 20-30% and erase 2026E profits.
- **当前结论：** Inventory risk has been largely defused; Q1 is seasonal shipment peak, Q2 destocking is underway, contract liabilities growth signals dealer confidence, but not fully conclusive until H1 sales data.
- **采用证据：** KPE03: management states Q1 is inventory peak, Q2 destocking normal；EV012: contract liabilities 7.9 bn, +35.9% YoY；KPE04: channel inventory 2-3 months, Qinghua 20 destocking positive
- **最强反证：** Contract liabilities could reflect forced advance payments, not end-demand; real sell-through remains unobservable (only 30% scan rate).
- **模型/估值影响：** No impairment to revenue forecast; we keep base 2026E revenue at 36.1 bn, but widen bear scenario range.
- **置信度与下次验证：** medium；Q2 2026 contract liabilities and revenue in H1 report.

- **核心证据：** EV011: net cash 12.5 bn, zero interest-bearing debt, DPS 6.56 CNY; market cap 133.7 bn, EV 121.2 bn.
- **最强反证：** If profits drop below 8 bn and dividend is halved, floor shifts to 70 CNY; not visible yet.
- **财务传导：** Floor value = net cash + bear case earnings power (8.5 bn) × 10x PE, implying ~850-950 CNY bn market cap.
- **市场定价：** Current price 109.56 CNY equates to 133.7 bn market cap, already 40% above this floor, suggesting the market is not pricing total collapse.
- **证伪条件：** Net profit below 8 bn or DPS below 4 CNY in 2027 would breach the floor thesis.
- **当前裁决：** partial

### 论点2：Channel health (contract liabilities +35.9%, 2-3 month inventory) indicates the downturn is a manageable cycle, not a structural brand erosion.

- **关键问题：** Is the brand losing traction with distributors and end consumers?
- **核心证据：** EV012 contract liabilities; KPE03 management commentary; KPE04 stable Qinghua 20 sell-through.
- **最强反证：** Qinghua 30 weak demand and rebates could spill over to brand perception; consumer scan rate coverage only 30%.
- **财务传导：** Healthy channels prevent a revenue cliff and support base case revenue -6.9% YoY.
- **市场定价：** Multiple contraction to 12x PE already prices a cyclical earnings decline, not a brand demise; if channel health holds, multiple can rebound.

- **当前裁决：** partial
