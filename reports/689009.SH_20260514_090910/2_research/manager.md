Ninebot’s earnings just fell off a cliff. Its low beta is a statistical artifact of its own idiosyncratic crash, not structural defensiveness. In a broad sell-off, capital seeks cash or bonds first, then defensive yield. A broken-down consumer discretionary growth stock is far down the rotation list. The “relative value magnet” logic is an unverifiable hope, not a tactical edge.
