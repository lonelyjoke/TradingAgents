### Core Thesis & Expectation Gap

The core bet embedded in 002202.SZ’s current price is that Q1 2026’s margin inflection (16.8% gross margin) is durable, operating cash flow will turn decisively positive in Q2, and the 53.9 GW order book (including 9.6 GW overseas) will drive a multi-quarter earnings upgrade cycle. The **expectation gap is unfavorable**: the market has already priced this full-throated scenario into a PE of 34x (85.7th percentile) and PB of 2.66 (94.7th percentile). Verified evidence from Q1 contradicts the bull thesis—the cost-revenue spiral is real and unresolved (cost of revenue +73.95% vs revenue +63.48%), and OCF remains deeply negative at -1.7x net income. The stock is discounting perfection; any disappointment will trigger multiple compression.

### Probability / Payoff / Conviction

- **Bear probability (~60%)**: Fragility dominates. The margin spike is a timing artifact, cash conversion stays negative, and earnings fail to sustain upgrades. Downside: -20 to -30% from current 25.09 toward 200-day SMA (19.51).
- **Bull probability (~20%)**: All the unverified assumptions (Q2 OCF turn, sustainable >15.5% gross margin with cost deceleration) materialize. Upside: +15-20% toward recent highs.
- **Neutral/sideways (~20%)**: Mixed data keeps stock range-bound.

**Conviction: High** (based on verifiable Q1 cost/revenue scissors and cash conversion strain, supported by same-industry peer analysis favoring 三一重能 (688349.SH) and the Operators segment).

002202.SZ sits in a **high valuation / high (but fragile) prosperity** setup. Revenue growth is strong, but margins are under cost pressure and cash conversion is breaking down. The market has assigned a premium that assumes the prosperity is durable and high quality. Our calibration finds this premium unjustified given the visible strain in Q1. The stock is not a deep value play; it is a cyclical peak-risk name with earnings quality concerns.
