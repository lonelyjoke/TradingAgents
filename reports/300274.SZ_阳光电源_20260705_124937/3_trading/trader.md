### Investment Thesis

Sungrow is in a temporary earnings trough (Q1 2026 net profit –40% YoY, ESS shipment flat YoY, unit profit at 0.11 元/Wh) driven by US tariff uncertainty, competitive pricing pressure, and the seasonal shape of a large order book. The thesis rests on three verifiable recovery paths:

1. **ESS unit profit normalization** – Q1 gross margin already improved to ~30% QoQ (EV042), driven by higher-margin European mix. European 3-year CAGR revised to +60% (KPE04); long-duration (8-hr) demand is emerging. The 122亿 contract liabilities and 40亿 prepayments (EV018, EV043) confirm a heavy H2 delivery schedule. **Evidence strength:** medium (awaiting Q2 conversion rate).

2. **AIDC ESS commercialization** – Hundreds of MWh backlog, Google/Meta certification (KPE06). Policy (15th Five-Year Plan, green transition) supports data centre energy demand. **Evidence strength:** low (no revenue in filings; certification ≠ orders).

3. **SST product launch** – July 9 commercial release; 300-person R&D team, 5亿 annual spend (KPE04). **Evidence strength:** low (pre-revenue; watch for customer announcements post-launch).

**Key bear risks (verified in filings and Q&A):**
- US tariffs at 38.4%, safe harbour only through 2028H1; future US demand may be pulled forward (EV042).
- Q1 ESS shipment flat YoY – no volume acceleration yet (KPE06).
- AIDC/SST zero current revenue – valuation premium implies optionality that may take years to monetise.

**Expectation gap:** The market seems to price the trough earnings (PE TTM 21.9x, market cap ~2,950亿) without giving full weight to the order book recovery or the optionality. Our core fair value of 3,600亿 (20x 2027E EPS 8.68元) implies ~22% upside. If AIDC/SST deliver any revenue by 2028, upside could reach +35–40%.

**Falsification signals:**
- H1 2026 ESS unit profit stays ≤0.11 元/Wh → recovery delayed → cut rating to Hold.
- Q2 revenue conversion <20% QoQ → contract liabilities may be defensive or cancellable → reduce position.
- SST launch attracts only small pilots → optionality value →0.

### Position Sizing & Risk Management

- **Entry level:** Current price ~142 元/股 (implied by PE TTM 21.9x). Initiate at **Overweight with 3–5% portfolio weight** (depending on risk budget).  
- **Add on weakness:** If price falls to 120–130 元 (20–22x trough earnings), increase to 5–7% weight, as downside is limited by order book and net cash.  
- **Profit-taking / trimming zones:**  
  - **1/3 trim** at 165–170 元 (18–20x 2027E EPS, our core fair value initial leg).  
  - **1/3 trim** at 200–210 元 (20x 2028E EPS) if AIDC/SST optionality begins to materialise.  
  - **Hold remainder** through 2027E to capture full recovery + optionality payoff.  
- **Stop / re-evaluate triggers:**  
  - H1 2026 ESS unit profit <0.11 元/Wh → reduce to neutral-weight (Hold).  
  - Q2 revenue conversion <20% QoQ → cut to 2% weight and re-evaluate.  
  - US FCC ban expansion beyond safe harbour → shift to Underweight.

**Cycle-valuation calibration:**  
Low valuation / low prosperity setup. The current trough earnings and depressed sentiment create an asymmetric payoff if recovery materialises. The balance sheet is resilient (net cash, no refinancing risk), so the company can survive a prolonged trough. Marginal signals (contract liabilities, prepayments, Europe demand upgrade) support a cyclical recovery thesis.

### Conclusion

Sungrow offers a recoverable earnings trough with a strong order book, a resilient balance sheet, and free optionality from AIDC/SST. The market appears to discount near-term weakness without full credit for H2 delivery ramp and structural demand tailwinds. Risk/reward favours the bull case if the recovery thesis holds; the three falsification signals provide an exit route if it fails.
