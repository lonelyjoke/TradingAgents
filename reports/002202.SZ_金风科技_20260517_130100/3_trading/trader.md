## Trading Decision: 002202.SZ (Goldwind Science & Technology)

**Rating: SELL (Underweight)**

**Current Price:** ~25 CNY  
**Key Evidence:** TTM P/E ~34x; Q1 2026 contract liabilities declining; FCF –7.0bn CNY in recent period; institutional shareholder reduction from 197k to 693k retail holders; EU FSR investigation unresolved.

**Core Bet (Bearish):** The market is overpaying for a “volume-price-margin” recovery that is already showing signs of slowing new orders and structurally negative free cash flow. We bet that the market’s optimistic expectations (implied 34x P/E) are unsustainable as focus shifts from shipment growth to order deceleration and cash burn.

**Expectation Gap:** The market prices a V-shaped margin recovery and flawless overseas/20MW execution. In contrast, the only verifiable leading indicators (contract liabilities down, new order growth trailing deliveries) suggest demand is softening, and the FCF deficit makes the high P/E vulnerable. The EU FSR risk is a material overhang that is not discounted.

**Catalyst Path:**  
- **Near-term (1-2 quarters):** 2026 H1 results – if gross margin <16% or new order growth remains weak, de-rating likely.  
- **Medium-term (6-12 months):** EU FSR outcome – any penalty or access restriction would severely damage the overseas growth narrative.

**Falsification Signals (would reverse SELL):**  
1. Sustained acceleration in order backlog and new orders (quarter-over-quarter, above industry).  
2. Operating cash flow turns positive and FCF (OCF – capex) narrows significantly for two consecutive quarters.  
3. EU FSR resolves without material penalty.  
4. Gross margin sustainably above 18% driven by product mix, not one-off asset sales.

**Next Verification Points:**  
- 2026 semi-annual report (Aug. 2026) – focus on margin, contract liabilities, cash flow.  
- Monthly/quarterly operating data for new order trends.  
- EU FSR investigation progress updates.

**Position Sizing:** Reduce existing positions aggressively. For new shorts (if permitted), allocate 2–3% of portfolio with tight stop-loss at ~28 CNY (where the story could revive on hype). For long-term portfolios, switch to healthier balance-sheet alternatives (e.g., 600089.SH).

**Re-entry Watch Range (for potential long re-opening):**  
- **Estimated fair range: 15–20 CNY**  
- **Basis:** This range would imply a forward P/E of ~15–20x on a conservative mid-cycle EPS estimate (~1.0–1.2 CNY per share), where the FCF yield becomes positive and the EU risk is either resolved or fully discounted.  
- **Unverified key assumption:** The mid-cycle EPS figure is not explicitly disclosed; it is a rough inference from historical trough and the plan’s margin assumptions. Actual normalized EPS should be recalculated after H1 2026 data is released.  
- **Trigger for re-entry:** Confirmation of a durable FCF inflection, new order re-acceleration, or clear removal of the EU overhang, combined with the price entering this range.

**Cycle-Valuation Setup:** High valuation (34x P/E) / low prosperity (FCF negative, margins compressed). This is the most dangerous quadrant. The stock needs a near-perfect recovery to justify the price; any disappointment will cause a large multiple contraction. The bearish risk/reward is superior until verifiable improvement appears.

**Theme Valuation Bridges:**  
- **Overseas expansion (Tier-1):** Apply a significant risk discount until EU FSR is resolved. Do not value at full 9.6 GW backlog.  
- **20MW turbine (Tier-2):** Purely optional – no revenue/margin evidence. Zero weight in base valuation.  
- **Asset/lPO uplifts (Tier-2):** Treat as upside lottery, not base case.  
- **Data/hydrogen/space (Tier-3):** Zero valuation weight.

**Bottom Line:** Sell 002202.SZ. The stock’s current price embeds optimistic assumptions that are contradicted by weakening order momentum, deep cash consumption, and unresolved regulatory risk. Wait for a material derating or clear evidence of a sustainable turning point before considering re-entry.
