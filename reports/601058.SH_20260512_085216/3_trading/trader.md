### Core Bet & Expectation Gap  

The debate hinges on whether the market’s ~12.6× trailing PE is a **punitive discount to a mispriced earnings recovery** (bull) or a **rational discount for a structurally low‑return, capex‑intensive business** (bear).  

- **Bull bet:** Profit trough is behind; revenue growth + gross margin recovery will re‑rate the stock toward a growth multiple.  
- **Bear bet (which we favour):** The “volume‑up, profit‑thin” dynamic is entrenched. Low incremental margins, negative free cash flow, and unresolved trade/macro risks justify the current multiple.  

The **expectation gap** is small: the market is not pricing catastrophe but a realistic 0–5% profit growth path. The bull needs to prove acceleration to >10% net profit growth; the Q1 2026 print of +1.7% YoY does not clear that bar.
