## Investment Decision: 002202.SZ (Goldwind Sci & Tech)

**Rating: Overweight (Tactical Buy with Strict Risk Controls)**

### Core Bet

We are underwriting the **sustainability of Goldwind’s earnings improvement trend** – specifically that high revenue growth, sequential gross margin recovery (from reported 16.8% in 2025 vs depressed trough), and large contract liabilities will persist for at least 1–2 quarters, allowing current ~35x PE to narrow the discount to the electrical-equipment sector median (~45x). This is **not** a bet on a structural global-wind super-cycle; the Morocco project remains an unvalued option without project-level financial details.

### Expectation Gap

Market consensus is deeply split: bulls see a cyclical recovery turning structural; bears see a low-quality growth trap. The real gap is in **margin sustainability and cash flow quality**. Our plan explicitly acknowledges the uncertainty but assigns a positive expected value because verifiable top-line and backlog data strongly support near-term momentum, while the bear case relies on unverified assumptions about cash flow deterioration and one-off margin drivers.

### Key Evidence (Verified from Analyst Reports)

- **Revenue growth**: 2024 revenue ¥57.0B, +12.1% YoY; 2025 guidance ¥68.4B, +20% YoY – confirmed acceleration.
- **Gross margin**: Recovered to 16.8% in 2025 from trough ~13%. Breakdown into pricing vs cost mix is **unverified**, but the direction is confirmed.
- **Contract liabilities**: ¥27.4B (on ¥57B revenue) – strong forward order coverage.
- **Balance sheet pressure**: Debt-to-asset ratio 71.1%, current ratio 0.93, net debt ¥18.1B – structural but manageable for a growth-phase company.
- **Technical setup**: Share price ~¥26, near recent lows after sell-off; plan suggests waiting for stabilization signals (e.g., doji, low volume bounce) before adding positions.

### Missing Evidence (Critical Research Gaps – Cap Conviction)

- **Q1 2026 cash flow statement** – especially operating cash flow. This is the single most important missing piece to falsify the bear case.
- **Wind turbine bidding prices and raw material costs (steel, copper, rare earth)** – without these, the gross margin improvement cannot be decomposed into pricing power vs cost tailwind. This is the **core unverified assumption**.
- **Foreign project details** (Morocco) – absent financial exposure, timeline, or offtake contracts; treat as upside optionality only.

### Risk/Reward

- **Bullish probability**: 55–60% – based on high growth, margin recovery trend, and heavy contract liabilities.
- **Bearish probability**: 40–45% – driven by balance-sheet fragility, cash flow risk, and unverified margin drivers.
- **Upside**: 15–30% from ¥26 → ¥30–¥34 (reflecting PE re-rating to 40–45x if trend continues).
- **Downside**: 15–25% from ¥26 → ¥22–¥20 (PE compression to 30x or lower if growth quality is falsified).
- **Expected value**: Positive but modest – not the asymmetric bet some bulls claim.

### Action & Position Sizing

**Action: Buy (tactical, staged entry)**  
- Do **not** chase a bounce. Enter on further weakness or after clear stabilization (volume contraction, candle confirmation) around **¥25–26**.
- Initial position: **1/3 to 1/2 of normal full position** – keep dry powder for potential re-entry at better levels or for capital protection.
- **Stop-loss**: Hard stop at **¥24**. A decisive close below this level breaks both the recent technical support and the psychological buyback floor. If triggered, reduce total exposure to neutral or underweight.
- **Profit-taking range**: **¥30–34**. Scale out 1/3 at ¥30, 1/3 at ¥32–33, hold the rest for the 15–30% re-rating scenario. Do not hold beyond ¥34 without fresh catalyst.

### Key Verification Catalysts

1. **Q1 2026 earnings release** (next ~2 months) – focus on:
   - Operating cash flow (must not show large sequential negative beyond normal seasonal patterns).
   - Gross margin breakdown (any segment disclosure to confirm sustainability).
   - Contract liability vs receivables relationship.
2. **Industry pricing data** – any independent wind turbine tender price data or raw material index moves will strengthen or weaken conviction.
3. **Share price reaction to stop-loss level** – if price tests ¥24 on higher volume, treat as a falsification signal and exit.

### Conviction Level: Moderate

We have direction but not conviction on the scale of the payoff. The missing micro-level evidence (cash flow, price/cost decomposition) prevents us from rating this a high-conviction buy. **This is a disciplined, evidence-limited trade – not a core long.** Use size and stop-loss to manage the 40–45% probability of the bear case materializing.
