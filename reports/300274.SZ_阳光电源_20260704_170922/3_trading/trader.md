### Core Investment Thesis (核心投资论点)

The core bet is whether H2 2026 revenue acceleration validates Q1 2026 profit decline as cyclical rather than structural. Three variables determine the outcome:

1. **Contract liability conversion** – ¥12.2bn contract liabilities must convert into H2 revenue at 50-55% rate (¥6-6.7bn) with gross margin above 30%
2. **Energy storage gross margin sustainability** – Q1 2026 storage margin of ~30% must hold under regional mix improvement despite domestic price wars
3. **Operating expense dilution** – Q1 expense ratio of ~14.7% must fall as H2 revenue accelerates, but RMB appreciation (3.32% in Q2 2026) creates headwinds for financial expenses

**Expectation gap**: Current price of ¥126.16 implies ~27x 2026E P/E on ~¥9.5bn net profit, which already discounts a moderate recovery but not acceleration. The gap between current valuation and "normalized 2027E" scenario (18x P/E on ¥11.25bn = ¥83.5/share core value) creates asymmetric downside risk if H2 proof fails.

**Catalyst path**: H1 2026 report (August) will show contract liability drawdown, storage segment margin, expense trajectory, and working capital trends.

**Falsification signals**: (1) H1 contract liabilities fall >10% sequentially, (2) storage gross margin <28%, (3) operating margin <16% despite stable revenue, (4) working capital ratio continues rising.

**Position implication**: No conviction to buy at current levels; no evidence to sell. Hold with positive watch.
